Extranet

An extranet is a private network that extends an organization's intranet to provide secure access to information and resources for authorized users outside the organization, such as partners, suppliers, or customers.

Extranets are built on the same technologies as intranets but with additional security measures to ensure that only authorized users can access the information. They allow organizations to collaborate with external parties while maintaining control over their internal resources.

Overall, extranets play a vital role in facilitating communication and collaboration between organizations and their external partners, enabling them to share information and resources securely.
